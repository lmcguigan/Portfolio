Let's make something

Hi, I'm Lucy!

I love to make things! Projects that afford the opportunity to leverage my skills in web development, design, and illustration are very exciting to me. Words, images, and interactive components multiply their magnitude when they work seemlessly in tandem.

I bring a diverse background and perspective, informed by experience in development at a nonprofit community design center and various roles for museum exhibitions.

I received a Master's of Arts in Art History from Southern Methodist University and a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from the University of Pennsylvania.
